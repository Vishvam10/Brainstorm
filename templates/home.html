Brainstorm is a flashcard app that will boost your memory and make you remember important things quickly.

Features

Create a Deck

With just one click of button, you can create a custom deck !

This can have a custom name and can hold unlimited questions

Play

In every turn, you get a random flashcard from the deck. Try to recollect the correct answers !

Performance Analysis

Get a report of your overall scores. This includes average and the standard deviation of it. You also get a recommendation for revising certain decks.

Deck-Wise Analysis

Get a graphical view of your deck-wise perfomance and focus on improving on the harder decks

Deck Management

You have full freedom ! You can create, update and delete cards and decks as and when you require.
